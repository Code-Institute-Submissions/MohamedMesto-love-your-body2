Why Love Fasting With Us?

Sunrise - Start - End Fasting

Fasting typically begins at dawn or sunrise, marking the start of the day. Devotees abstain from food and drink until sunset during religious or spiritual fasts..

the Right way to break the fasting

The recommended method to break a fast is with a light meal, incorporating water-rich fruits, vegetables, and hydrating fluids to ease digestion and prevent discomfort.

Animals and insects are also fasting

In the natural world, various animals undergo periods of fasting as part of their life cycles. Hibernating animals like bears abstain from eating during winter months, relying on stored fat for sustenance. Insects such as certain species of ants may exhibit fasting behavior during unfavorable environmental conditions, conserving energy until more suitable circumstances arise.

Best Acts of Worship during Fasting

Acts of worship during fasting often include prayer, reading Quran, reflection, and acts of kindness. Embracing patience and gratitude enhances the spiritual experience..
